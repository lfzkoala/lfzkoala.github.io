I'm a cryptographer building at the intersection of cryptography and blockchain with help of my many AI agents. My mission is to bring crypto to the mainstream through privacy.

### What I Do

I research and build cryptographic systems. My work has shipped at **Coinbase** (leading the open-source [Kryptology](https://github.com/coinbase/kryptology) library and **IBM** (contributing to the [Hyperledger Fabric](https://openblockchain.readthedocs.io/en/latest/protocol-spec/) protocol). I've published at venues including IEEE CSF and EUROCRYPT, and reviewed for CRYPTO, ASIACRYPT, PKC and IEEE TIFS. More details on my [Research](/research.md) page. 

### Investing

I invest in crypto, AI, security and quantum. More details on my [Investing](/investing) page.
